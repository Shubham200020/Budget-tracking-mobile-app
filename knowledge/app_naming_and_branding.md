## 2. Naming Suggestions for Our App

We want a name that sounds **premium, modern, and trustworthy**, moving away from generic local names to something highly brandable.

### 🏆 Option 1: skmBudgex (Selected & Implemented)
* **Rationale:** Blends the custom prefix `skm` (from the package `com.example.skmaccount`) with "Budgex" (Budget tracker). Memorable, unique, and tech-forward.
* **Slogan:** *Smart budgeting, automated.*

### 🏆 Option 2: FinFlow
* **Rationale:** A fast, tech-forward portmanteau of "Budget" and "Tracker/Exchange". Very short, memorable, and fits a developer/power-user audience.
* **Slogan:** *Smart budgeting, automated.*

### 🌱 Option 3: RupeeRise (India Market Target)
* **Rationale:** Plays on the localized currency (INR) and the emotional goal of making your savings grow.
* **Slogan:** *Watch your savings grow.*
